What is knowledge work?

The first thought of knowledge work is that it involves typing...

But the right definition of a knowledge worker probably closer to: "someone for whom a unit of knowledge can be leveraged for increased economic output." 

For example, the manager of a warehouse can read an essay on message passing and create a more efficient warehouse...

But, a notary cannot necessarily do their job more efficiently by virtue of greater knowledge.

Since learning is the acquisition of knowledge, learning is essential to gaining leverage in knowledge work.

We Focus on Learning to Play Games.

Why?

We're focused on learning acceleration on games for two reasons.

1. They present an interesting opportunity to map what the game AI perceives of the human cognitive progress to simultaneous neurological sensing.

We can thereby learning how large improvements in perceived ability were preceded by various neurological signals.

2. Since many human activities can be recast as games, we believe that if we can learn how humans can learn games best we can generalize to other areas.

We choose Practical over Theoretical
