Topos Mission

We are dedicated to creating the best environment for life-long learning. Too often, the gradient of learning goes to zero after college. Topos exists to ensure that everyone at any age (0-90+) is well-motivated and well-resourced to continue learning.

To achieve that goal, we have created Topos house which hosts 6-12 scientists at a time from deliberately disparate fields of science and art with the hope (now supported by strong evidence) that the greatest collaborations start over the kitchen table instead of conference table or even lecture tables.

Sunday Dinners

Every Sunday, we host a dinner for 8-14 people focused around a central topic. We usually following Thomas Jefferson's rules for social dinners. We've hosted 100+ in a row.

Seminars

Peer-Run Learning Groups

We host weekly seminars on a variety of topics: symplectic geometric tour of mathematical physics, thermodynamics underlying Jeremy England's work, ancient Indian economic history etc... We prioritize people presenting their learning.
